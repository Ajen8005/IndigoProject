Wolf Splicing

The character gains 2 Speed, and Automatically unlocks the Teamwork skill. The character develops a concealable tail and ears, but has a -2 penalty to all Innocence and Intimidation checks. Wolf-characters are especially lethal, allowing them to apply a Bleed effect whenever they deal melee damage, dealing 3 additional damage at the start of the opponentâ€™s turn, for 3 turns.

Bear Splicing

The character gains 1 Aggression and Max HP, loses 2 Compassion, and gains a + 1 bonus to Brawling checks, even if not unlocked. Your body develops visible bear ears that are difficult to conceal, and any other requested attributes. Bear spliced characters also have inhuman strength allowing them to deal an additional 2 melee damage and lift things they normally could not.

Elephant Splicing

The character gains a bonus 4 Max HP, but loses 2 Aggression. An elephant never forgets, so they may perform Copying attempts at things they have seen at least once, and it does not need to be recently (requires Copying to be unlocked.) The character also receives a -2 penalty to all Stealth and Deceit checks.

Stalk Splicing

Having been spliced with some plant with a hefty stalk, your character gains +1 Hit Prevention, and can freely heal 2 HP each turn if standing on soil (can be used out of combat as well, to heal to essentially full.) The stalkâ€™s strength though is limited, so this character suffers from - 2 Max HP. Aloe Splicing

Having been sliced with Aloe Vera, your hands have a healing touch to them, allowing you to heal allies up to 4 HP each turn by touching them (this does not work out of combat.) This character gains +1 cyber_textiousness, +1 Order, and - 2 Speed. Being part of a versatile plant has provided you a +1 bonus to Gene Splicing checks, even if not unlocked.
